PRIVACY POLICY

Village Defender Standard Edition

This Privacy Policy explains how the Village Defender mobile game collects, uses, and protects user data. Our game now uses a third-party advertising service (LevelPlay Mediation).

1 Data Collected

Village Defender Standard Edition does not directly collect Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number for the core function of the game. However, certain non-personal data is automatically collected through the third-party advertising and analytics services used by the app.

2 Third-Party Services (LevelPlay Mediation)

Our game uses the Unity LevelPlay Mediation (ironSource) service for ad delivery and revenue generation. This service collects and processes the non-personal data listed above to optimize advertisements.

LevelPlay Mediation shares this data with its integrated networks (ironSource Ads, Unity Ads) and other ad partners it mediates (such as AdMob, Meta, etc., if connected).

This data collection is necessary for personalized advertising (where applicable) and for the developer to maximize ad revenue.

3 Children's Privacy

Village Defender Standard Edition is suitable for players of all ages.

If our app targets children, the advertising services are configured to comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and other relevant regulations.

Nevertheless, parents are encouraged to check their device settings to help protect their children's privacy.

4 Data Security and Retention

All data collected via third-party services is transmitted through secure channels (HTTPS).

User data is retained in accordance with the policies of LevelPlay and its mediated networks, for as long as necessary to fulfill business and legal requirements.

5 Data Deletion Requests

Users have the right to request the deletion of data (such as Advertising ID, Device ID, etc.) collected about them by the app.
